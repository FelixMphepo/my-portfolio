ACTIVE FAULT MAPPING


The Geological Mapping and Mineral Assessment of Malawi (GEMMAP) financed
by France for the Government of Malawi and led by the BRGM, aims to
update the country's geological mapping. The project integrates
different international partiners:GTK of Finland,CGS of South Africa

The GEMMAP project includes natural hazards mapping
(floods, landslides,earthquakes and active faults). The aim of the active
fault analysis is to give a first information about the location of
the location of the active of potentially active faults in the
whole country of Malawi.

ACTIVE FAULT ASSESSMENT: METHODOLOGY


1.Compilation of the available scientific data(publications, DTM,
satellite imagery,earthquake cataloque) and analysis of seismotectonic
regional context.

2.On a GIS, interpretative map of potentially active faults: Extracted
from bibliography(earthquake,surface breaks,quaternary deformation,
other indicatives data) or deduced from GIS analysis(escarpment,contact formations,
seismic profiles).
